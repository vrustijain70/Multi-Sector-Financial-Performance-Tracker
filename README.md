## Multi-Sector Financial Performance Tracker
Analyzed 8 quarters of financial data across 5 companies — 
HUL, Asian Paints, HDFC Bank, Tata Motors, Sun Pharma.

**Tools used:** Excel | Power BI | Screener.in

**Sectors covered:** FMCG | Paints | Banking | Auto | Pharma

**Deliverables:**
- Quarterly data collection & QoQ calculations
- OPM, NPM, Revenue Growth analysis
- Power BI dashboard
- 1-page equity research note

**Key Findings:**
- Sun Pharma leads on margins (avg OPM 30%)
- Tata Motors leads on revenue growth (avg 8.99% QoQ)
- HDFC Bank showed most consistent profitability
- Asian Paints showed stagnant revenue amid rising competition
